----------------
Mechanic Changes
----------------

For any mechanic that's not described in here, assume a wild mix of Gen4 and Gen9 mechanics.

Easy mode will reduce enemy trainer level by 1 for lvl <= 30, and 2 by lvl > 30. 
Button L will toggle the repel.
Battle style is forced to set.
Bag is unacessible during trainer battles.
Gym Trainers and Gym Leaders will heal your party to full before battle.
EVs are removed from the game.
TMs are consumed on use.
HMs do not need to be taught, obtaining them plus the required badge for use is enough.
Rock Climb does not require a specfic Badge.
Whirlpool/Waterfall require a Water Pokemon.
Fly works between Johto and Kanto.

Critical hit chance: 1/16.
Critical hit damage multiplier: 1.5.

Protean/Libero work as Gen8.
Defiant/Competitive can be triggered by allys.
Hail has been replaced by Snow.
Weather abilities/moves: Will set Weather permanently.
Terrain abilities/moves: Will set Terrain permanently. Cannot be removed by Defog.

Held items: Items that get consumed or removed from your Pokémon in any way during battle will not be restored.

Apricorn trees can be harvested once, to obtain Berries.

The starter choice determines a few unique traits and gifts throughout the game.

Level Cap
Once a Pokémon reaches a level cap the Infinite Candy item will no longer work and the Pokémon will not gain any experience in battles or Day Care.
The level cap is tied boss fights and raised as you defeat them, progressing through the story.
The boss fights are noted in documentation and are also noticeable by the battle transition as they start.
You can overcome the cap by using Rare Candies, but those are rare and limited.
